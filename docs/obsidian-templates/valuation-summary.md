## 🔢 Methodology

> [!note] How Preço Teto Is Calculated
> Five methods averaged (where data available):
> 1. **Bazin** — DPS ÷ 6% (dividend yield floor)
> 2. **Graham Clássico** — √(22.5 × LPA × VPA)
> 3. **Graham Moderno** — (LPA × (8.5 + 2g) × 4.4) ÷ NTN-B yield
> 4. **Múltiplos P/L** — sector fair P/E × LPA
> 5. **P/VP** — sector fair P/BV × VPA
>
> Composite = equal-weight average of available methods.
> **Preço Teto = Composite × 0.80** (20% margin of safety).
